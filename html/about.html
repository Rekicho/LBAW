About KEYVALUE

Nowadays, with the growth of the internet and delivery services, we feel that most people shouldn't need to travel to the closest big city in order to buy what they want. Furthermore, there are products that we can't even find in our local shops. Online shopping is the future.

Users can navigate our catalog using an advanced search feature, save their favorite items and add them to a shopping cart. They can sign up or log in either by typing their personal information or just by using an OAuth method (Facebook and Google). They can later check their cart, remove any unwanted item and ultimately buy them, paying either offline or using another method, such as PayPal. The products will be grouped by their categories, brand, price, and popularity. In addition to that, the system will offer a responsive design capable of giving the best experience to the user, both in desktop, tablet or smartphone.
